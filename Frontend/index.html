Learning and Development (LGD) Management

Welcome to Learning and Development (LGD) Management

The project aims to develop a web-based platform that streamlines the management of training and development programs in large organizations. The platform allows account managers to request training programs, the LCD team (Admin) to create and manage courses, and employees to log in, complete courses, and provide feedback. The focus is on efficient training coordination, tracking employee progress, and enhancing the learning experience.
